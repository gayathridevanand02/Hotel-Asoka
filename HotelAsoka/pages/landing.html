Welcome to Hotel Asoka

A premier destination for travelers seeking comfort, elegance, and a taste of local hospitality. Nestled in the heart of the city, our hotel offers a serene escape with modern amenities and warm service, ensuring a memorable stay for all our guests.

Hotel Asoka has been a symbol of elegance and comfort since its establishment in 1975. Our journey began with a vision to provide exceptional hospitality and create memorable experiences for our guests.

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities to include luxurious rooms, state-of-the-art conference halls, and a world-class restaurant. Each addition has been thoughtfully designed to cater to the evolving needs of our guests, while maintaining the charm and character that defines Hotel Asoka.

Join us as we continue to write the next chapter in our storied history, offering unparalleled service and a warm, welcoming atmosphere in the heart of the city.
